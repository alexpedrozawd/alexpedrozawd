Dominaria Cards Leaked - A new Dinosaur!

A lot of Dominaria cards was leaked yesterday, and we have a new dinosaur



Yesterday a lot of Dominaria cards was leaked. Not with images, but a chinese text that reveals what every single card leaked do. You can see the list clicking here on MythicSpoiler.

An interesting thing that happened: a new dinosaur was revelad, and it rocks:



A 2GG dinosaurs 5/5 that can enter earlier because cards like Otepec Huntmaster, Drover of the Mighty and Kinjalli's Caller it's very good. The other ability it's strong too: 2G for kicker and this cards costs 7 mana, BUT when it enters on battlefield it fights against another creature. So, we have two options by playing this:

- Pay four (or less, and maybe with haste - Otepec Huntmaster) and put a 5/5 on field
- Pay 7 (i'm sure that we will not pay the entire cost because the rest of ramp/reduction costs cards) and put a 5/5 on battlefield fighting against any creature.

It's a dinosaur with a removal, and this is very important for a dinosaur deck.
