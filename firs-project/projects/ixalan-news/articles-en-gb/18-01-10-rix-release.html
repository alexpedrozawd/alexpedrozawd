Be Prepared for RIX Release

The last part of the Ixalan block will arrive this month: Rivals of Ixalan

Prepare yourself: Rivals of Ixalan will arrive this month!

The prerelease will be on 13-14 of this month, and RIX will be available on sales on january 19. Are you prepared to change your deck and put new cards? We have selected a few cards to show you how strong this extension will be, take a look according to your tribe:

DINOSAURS:

Since Ixalan was released, the most powerfull creatures was the dinosaurs. They are huge and have a lot of power/thoughness, and have a special abbility called "Enrage". On Rivals of Ixalan, we will see the Elder Dinosaurs, one for each color (blue, black, green, red and white). Above we can see the dinosaurs for Naya deck, since it's difficult to play the black and blue ones because of the 2 mana cost of each respective color. You can see them on our card database of Rivals of Ixalan, if you are interessed.

PIRATES:

Pirates, as well as dinosaurs, are one of the exclusive tribes in Ixalan block, and they are very agile. They are present in the colors of the Grixis tribe (blue, black and red) and you can do aggro, control, and have treasures that generate mana of any color when sacrificed. They may not have much power or thougness, but they infest the battlefield and are quite violent, always in constant attack or stealing spells from opponents. Rivals of Ixalan add new pirates who get along very well with synergy of aggro or control decks.

MERFOLK:

The mermaids are back! Present in the colors blue and green, the merfolk are masters in growth: they are not very strong, but together they are strong, besides they give bonuses to each other by putting +1/+1 counters that little by little grows, and they transform into giant creatures. In addition, some of them can't be blocked. It's not too difficult to fill the battlefield of them and deal with your opponents. With Rivals of Ixalan, we will have strong cards for this tribe, such as the legendary Kumena. Decks of merfolk can be competitive in the Standard in actual scenario.

VAMPIRES:

Like the merfolk, vampires already existed in the Magic and are back in Ixalan. They can stay in the sunshine smoothly and manage to gain life points easily, as well as infest the battlefield with 1/1 lifelink tokens. The strategy is similar to the merfolk: they are small, but in huge amount they give a lot of trouble. Instead of being unlockable and gaining counters like merfolks, some of them have deathtouch and cause opponents to lose life points and the controller gains life. With the launch of RIX, just like the other tribes, vampires gained strong additions, in black and white colors.

I hope you guys have enjoyed the article, and see you at Rivals of Ixalan!
