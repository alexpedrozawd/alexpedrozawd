Wizards of the Coast annouced Brawl Format

A new format announced: Welcome to Brawl, a mini Standard Commander



Today Wizards of the Coast surprised everyone announcing a new format based on Commander and Standard: Brawl.

Brawl have the same rules as Commander format, the only difference it's 3 things:

  • Your deck must have exactly 60 cards (59 cards + the commander), 1 copy of each
  • Your initial life total is 30 instead of 40
  • Only Standard legal cards are available to play into this new format

They announce this format demonstrating as an example one of the new legendary cards that will be released on Dominaria, the "Firesong and Sunspeaker" minotaurs. You can see the original post here, on official WOTC website.



That's said, we are excited to create and test new decks to play and have fun with them, we are already thinking about some commanders, one of each tribes to build Brawl decks:

Gishath, Sun's Avatar for dinosaurs, Admiral Beckett Brass for pirates, Kumena, Tyrant of Orazca for merfolk and Vona, Butcher of Magan for vampires.

What are you waiting for? Let's BRAWL!
