Rampaging Ferocidon Banned

New banned and restricted list from Wizards brings extinction to Rampaging Ferocidon

The Star of Extinction came earlier to this poor dinosaur.

Today, january 15th of 2018, Wizards of the Coast announced the new banned and restricted lists. The standard format had four cards banned and they will no longer be accepted in the format. Here is the cards:

  • Attune with AEther
  • Rogue Refiner
  • Rampaging Ferocidon
  • Ramunap Ruins

It's a great lost to dinosaurs lovers (like me) because this card fits pretty well on main deck or sideboard, depending to your play style. This was a salvation against life decks (like vampires decks) and token generator decks (Guess what? Vampires again. And merfolk) because players can't get life, and this ability breaks the game against that type of decks. How many times it saves my life? I know, it's easier to remove, but your opponent will spend one remove spell against him, and you can put the big guys on battlefield (like dinosaurs, for example)



I'll miss that card. I hope Wizards do not start banishing more dinosaurs, because we don't are competitive, banishing more of our poor creatures will be more than devastating.

Cya!
